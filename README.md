📊 Sales, Margin & Returns Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)
📌 Overview

This Power BI dashboard provides a full-cycle analysis of sales performance, focusing on:

Revenue & growth
Profitability (Margin)
Cost structure (COGS, Discount, Marketing)
Returns & operational efficiency
Customer payments & debt

It is designed for distribution / retail analytics, enabling deeper understanding of what drives profitability and where risks exist.

🎯 Key KPIs
Total Sales Amount
Total Sales Quantity
Total Discount
Margin (₾) & Margin %
Total Returned Amount & Quantity
Debt & Payment Turnover
